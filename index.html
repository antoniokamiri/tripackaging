About Us

We are now five years old and are recognised as a leading supplier of single face kraft for the flower industry.

We have one of the most experienced staff in the corrugate industry with a lot of product knowledge and are therefore able to offer quality products and packaging solutions.We keep to date with the changing trends in the packaging industry and visit the flower auction in Holland and flower sellers in the UK and USA. We offer professional support and training on cold room chain management.

We provide both plain and printed single face kraft rolls and sheets. The printing ranges from customer artworks to company logos. The material used is both brown and white kraft paper.
