Our Focus

We provide both plain and printed single face kraft rolls and sheets. The printing ranges from customer artworks to company logos. The material used is both brown and white kraft paper.

Single Faced Kraft (SFK) is the ideal choice when it comes to safeguarding the frail flowers against debasement. This thick layer acts as a bulwark against any kind of external shocks.

Our aim is to use the right packaging for the right variety so that the client always gets a fresh and damage-free flower.
